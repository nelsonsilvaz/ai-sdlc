# Marketing Manager - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Customer Data & Segmentation
1. What customer data do you currently collect and track?
2. How do you segment customers (by rental frequency, vehicle preference, location, demographics)?
3. Do you track customer lifetime value or profitability?
4. What customer information is most valuable for marketing purposes?
5. How do you identify high-value or loyal customers?

### Campaigns & Promotions
6. How do you plan and execute promotional campaigns?
7. What types of promotions do you offer (discounts, loyalty rewards, seasonal offers)?
8. How do you track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns?
9. Do you need the ability to create targeted promotions for specific customer segments?
10. How do you currently communicate promotions to customers?

### Customer Loyalty & Retention
11. Do you have a loyalty or rewards program, and how does it work?
12. How do you track repeat bookings and customer retention rates?
13. What strategies do you use to encourage repeat business?
14. How do you measure customer satisfaction and retention?

### Analytics & Reporting
15. What marketing metrics and KPIs are most important to track?
16. How do you measure campaign ROI?
17. What reports do you need to monitor marketing performance?
18. Do you need integration with analytics tools (Google Analytics, etc.)?
19. How frequently do you review marketing performance data?

### Channel Management
20. What marketing channels do you use (email, SMS, social media, direct mail)?
21. How do you coordinate messaging across different channels?
22. Do you need to track customer response rates by channel?
23. How do you manage customer communication preferences?

### Pricing & Promotions Strategy
24. How does pricing strategy impact your marketing messaging?
25. Do you need to test different pricing or promotional strategies?
26. How do you align marketing with seasonal demand patterns?
27. What competitor analysis do you currently perform?

### Customer Feedback & Voice
28. How do you currently collect customer feedback and reviews?
29. Do you need integration with review platforms or feedback systems?
30. How do you track customer satisfaction and Net Promoter Score?
31. How is customer feedback used to improve services?

### System Requirements & Integration
32. What CRM or marketing automation systems do you currently use?
33. Do you need the car rental system to integrate with email marketing tools?
34. What data exports do you need for external marketing analysis?
35. Would mobile access to customer and campaign data be useful?

### Market Intelligence
36. What competitive information do you monitor?
37. How do you track market trends in the car rental industry?
38. Do you need the system to provide market segment insights?
