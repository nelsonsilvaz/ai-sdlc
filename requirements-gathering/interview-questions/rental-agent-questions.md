# Rental Agent / Customer Service - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Customer Interaction & Booking
1. What is your typical customer booking process from initial inquiry to rental agreement?
2. How do you currently handle customer inquiries about vehicle availability and pricing?
3. What booking channels do you support (phone, website, in-person, mobile app)?
4. How do you manage reservations and hold times for customers?
5. What information do you need to collect from customers during the booking process?
6. How do you handle walk-in customers versus pre-booked customers?

### Vehicle Selection & Recommendations
7. What factors do customers typically consider when selecting a vehicle (price, size, features)?
8. How do you recommend vehicles based on customer needs?
9. Do you need to show vehicle availability in real-time to customers?
10. How do you handle situations where a preferred vehicle is unavailable?

### Rental Agreement & Documentation
11. What information is required on the rental agreement?
12. How do you currently capture rental terms and conditions?
13. What payment methods do you accept?
14. How do you handle different rental durations (hourly, daily, weekly, monthly)?
15. Do you need to generate and print rental agreements efficiently?

### Customer Communication
16. How do you communicate rental confirmations to customers?
17. Do you need to send reminders or notifications about rental details?
18. How do you handle customer inquiries after the booking is confirmed?
19. What communication preferences do customers have?

### Issue Resolution & Support
20. What are common customer issues or complaints you handle?
21. How do you resolve disputes or issues during rentals?
22. What authority do you have to offer discounts or adjustments?
23. How do you escalate issues to management?

### System Requirements & Tools
24. What information do you need quick access to during customer interactions?
25. How important is a user-friendly interface for rapid booking?
26. Do you need integration with payment processing systems?
27. What reports would help you track booking trends and customer satisfaction?
28. Would mobile/tablet access be beneficial for your role?

### Efficiency & Performance
29. What are the typical wait times for booking processing?
30. How many bookings do you typically handle per day?
31. What features would help reduce booking time and improve customer satisfaction?
