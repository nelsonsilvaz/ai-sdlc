# Operations Manager - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Process Management & Workflow
1. What are the key operational processes in your car rental business?
2. How do you currently manage the rental process from booking to return?
3. What steps are involved in vehicle check-out and check-in?
4. How do you handle exceptions or deviations from standard procedures?
5. What are the bottlenecks or inefficiencies in your current operations?

### Resource Allocation & Scheduling
6. How do you allocate staff across different locations and shifts?
7. What scheduling challenges do you face?
8. How do you manage peak demand periods?
9. Do you need predictive scheduling based on expected demand?
10. How do you track staff utilization and productivity?

### Quality Control & Vehicle Inspection
11. What is your process for vehicle inspection before rental?
12. How do you assess and document vehicle condition (pre-rental and post-rental)?
13. What damage assessment and documentation process do you use?
14. How do you handle damage claims and liability issues?
15. What is your standard for vehicle cleanliness and readiness?

### Compliance & Safety
16. What safety procedures must be followed during vehicle rental?
17. Are there specific compliance requirements for your rentals?
18. How do you ensure customer documentation is complete and compliant?
19. How do you handle liability and insurance documentation?
20. What audit trails or compliance records are required?

### Multi-Location Management
21. How many locations do you operate, and how do you manage them?
22. What visibility do you need across all locations?
23. How do you standardize processes across different branches?
24. How do you handle inter-location vehicle transfers?
25. What reporting do you need at the location and company level?

### Exception Handling & Problem Resolution
26. What are common operational issues or exceptions?
27. How do you currently resolve rental disputes?
28. What is the escalation process for operational issues?
29. How do you handle customer complaints or returns?
30. What data do you need to investigate operational problems?

### Performance Monitoring & KPIs
31. What operational metrics do you track (rental throughput, vehicle turnover time)?
32. How do you measure operational efficiency?
33. What are the key performance indicators for your operations?
34. How do you identify areas for process improvement?
35. What benchmarks do you use for operational performance?

### Technology & Tools
36. What systems do you currently use for operations management?
37. How important is real-time visibility into operations?
38. Do you need mobile/field access for staff during operations?
39. What reporting and dashboards would help with decision-making?
40. How do you currently share information with staff and other departments?

### Integration & Data Flow
41. What existing systems need to integrate with the car rental system?
42. How do you currently manage data flow between different departments?
43. What data exports or reports do you need regularly?
44. How do you handle system downtime or contingency planning?

### Training & Documentation
45. How do you train staff on rental procedures?
46. What documentation or procedures need to be available in the system?
47. How do you keep procedures updated as processes change?
