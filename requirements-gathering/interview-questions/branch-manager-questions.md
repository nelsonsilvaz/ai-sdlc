# Branch Manager - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Branch Operations Overview
1. What are your main responsibilities as a branch manager?
2. How many rental agents and support staff do you manage?
3. What is the average daily rental volume at your branch?
4. What are your branch's primary customer segments?
5. How does your branch differ from other branches in terms of operations or clientele?

### Daily Operations & Workflow
6. What is a typical day like in terms of operational tasks and priorities?
7. How do you currently manage branch inventory and vehicle availability?
8. What processes do you oversee to ensure smooth daily operations?
9. How do you handle rush periods or peak demand times?
10. What issues typically arise during daily operations?

### Staff Management & Productivity
11. How do you manage and track staff performance?
12. What training do your staff need, and how do you provide it?
13. How do you handle staff scheduling and workload management?
14. What metrics do you use to measure staff productivity?
15. How do you motivate staff to provide excellent customer service?

### Revenue & Performance Goals
16. What revenue targets or goals does your branch have?
17. How do you track progress toward branch goals?
18. What factors impact your branch's revenue performance?
19. How do you analyze and report on branch financial performance?
20. What incentives or goals drive staff performance?

### Customer Service & Satisfaction
21. How do you ensure consistent quality customer service?
22. What is your process for handling customer complaints or issues?
23. How do you monitor customer satisfaction at your branch?
24. What are the most common customer complaints or issues?
25. How do you balance customer satisfaction with operational efficiency?

### Inventory & Resource Management
26. How do you manage vehicle inventory at your branch?
27. What happens when you have insufficient vehicles to meet demand?
28. How do you coordinate with other branches for vehicle transfers?
29. How do you forecast demand and plan inventory needs?
30. What visibility do you have into fleet utilization at your branch?

### Local Decision Making & Authority
31. What decisions can you make independently at the branch level?
32. What decisions require approval from higher management?
33. How do you handle pricing or promotional decisions?
34. What authority do you have regarding customer accommodations or adjustments?

### Multi-Location Coordination
35. How do you coordinate with other branches?
36. What information do you need to share with headquarters?
37. How do you stay aligned with company policies and procedures?
38. What support do you need from the central office?

### Reporting & Analytics
39. What reports do you need to generate or submit?
40. What metrics are most important for your branch's success?
41. How frequently do you report on branch performance?
42. What dashboards or visualizations would help with branch management?
43. How do you identify and address branch-specific performance issues?

### System Requirements & Tools
44. What information do you need quick access to for decision-making?
45. How important is real-time visibility into branch operations?
46. Do you need mobile access to branch management tools?
47. What reports or exports do you generate regularly?
48. How do you currently communicate with staff and other branches?

### Compliance & Quality Control
49. What compliance or quality standards must your branch maintain?
50. How do you ensure branch compliance with company policies?
51. What audit or inspection processes are in place?
52. How do you handle compliance issues or deviations?

### Growth & Improvement
53. What opportunities do you see for improving branch operations?
54. How do you identify areas for cost reduction or efficiency?
55. What would help you better manage and grow your branch?
