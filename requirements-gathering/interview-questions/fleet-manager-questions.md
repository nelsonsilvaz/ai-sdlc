# Fleet Manager - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Vehicle Management & Inventory
1. How do you currently track and manage your vehicle fleet?
2. What information do you need to capture for each vehicle (make, model, year, VIN, license plate, mileage, condition)?
3. How do you classify vehicles (economy, premium, SUV, van, etc.), and are there pricing tiers associated with each class?
4. What is your process for tracking vehicle maintenance schedules and history?
5. How do you handle vehicle wear and tear assessments?
6. Do you need to track GPS/telematics data for vehicles, and if so, what metrics are important?
7. How frequently do vehicles need to be serviced, and how do you schedule maintenance?

### Vehicle Status & Availability
8. How do you currently track which vehicles are available for rental, rented, or under maintenance?
9. Do you need real-time visibility into vehicle locations and status?
10. What happens when a vehicle needs emergency repairs while rented out?
11. How do you handle vehicle returns and inspection processes?

### Logistics & Operations
12. How many locations/branches do you operate, and how is the fleet distributed across them?
13. Do you need to track vehicle transfers between locations?
14. What is your process for managing vehicle shortages in specific locations?
15. How do you currently handle vehicle cleaning and preparation between rentals?
16. What metrics do you use to measure fleet utilization and efficiency?

### Reporting & Analytics
17. What reports do you need regularly (utilization rates, maintenance costs, vehicle condition status)?
18. How do you track vehicle depreciation and lifecycle costs?
19. What KPIs are most critical for your decision-making?

### Integration & System Requirements
20. What existing systems need to integrate with the new car rental system?
21. Do you need mobile access to fleet management information?
22. How important is automated alerting for maintenance overdue or vehicle condition issues?
