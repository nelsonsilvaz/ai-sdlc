# Finance / Accounting - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Revenue & Pricing Management
1. How do you currently manage rental pricing and rate structures?
2. Do you offer dynamic pricing based on demand, season, or vehicle type?
3. How are discounts and promotional offers applied to rentals?
4. What is your process for handling price changes and updates?
5. How do you track revenue by vehicle class, location, or customer segment?

### Payment Processing & Collections
6. How do you currently process rental payments?
7. What payment methods do you support?
8. How do you handle deposits and damage waivers?
9. What is your payment reconciliation process?
10. How do you manage late payments or payment disputes?
11. Do you need automated invoicing and billing?

### Financial Reporting & Analysis
12. What financial reports are required for management and stakeholders?
13. How do you calculate profitability by vehicle, location, or rental type?
14. What metrics do you track for revenue analysis (daily revenue, average rental value, occupancy rates)?
15. How frequently do you need financial reports?
16. What is your month-end and year-end financial closing process?

### Costs & Expenses
17. How do you track and allocate vehicle maintenance costs?
18. How do you manage insurance costs and track coverage?
19. What other operational expenses need to be tracked?
20. How do you handle fuel costs for vehicles?
21. Do you need to track depreciation and vehicle lifecycle costs?

### Accounts Receivable & Payable
22. How do you manage customer invoices and payment tracking?
23. What is your process for handling overdue payments?
24. How do you manage vendor payments for maintenance and services?
25. Do you need automated payment reminders or collection notices?

### Compliance & Audit
26. What compliance and audit requirements exist for the rental business?
27. How do you maintain financial records for tax purposes?
28. What retention policies apply to financial data?
29. Are there specific data validation or audit trail requirements?

### System Integration
30. What existing financial systems need to integrate with the car rental system?
31. Do you use accounting software (QuickBooks, SAP, etc.) that needs integration?
32. What export formats are needed for financial data (CSV, Excel, PDF)?
33. How important is real-time financial data visibility?

### Analytics & Decision Support
34. What dashboards or KPIs would help with financial decision-making?
35. Do you need forecasting or budget tracking capabilities?
36. How do you analyze profitability trends?
