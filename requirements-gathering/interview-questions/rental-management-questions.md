# Rental Management - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Rental Policy & Terms
1. What are your standard rental policies and terms?
2. How do you manage different rental durations (hourly, daily, weekly, monthly)?
3. What age and license requirements do you have for renters?
4. What insurance coverage and options do you offer?
5. How do you handle driver restrictions or blacklisted customers?

### Reservation & Booking Management
6. How do you manage reservation hold times and expiration?
7. What is your policy for cancellations and refunds?
8. How do you handle overbooking situations?
9. What modifications can be made to existing reservations?
10. How do you manage advanced bookings versus same-day rentals?

### Rental Agreement & Documentation
11. What information must be captured in a rental agreement?
12. What are the legal requirements for rental documentation?
13. How do you ensure customers understand rental terms and conditions?
14. What signatures or acknowledgments are required?
15. How do you handle rental agreement compliance and audit?

### Vehicle Assignment & Allocation
16. How do you assign vehicles to customers?
17. What factors determine vehicle assignment (vehicle class, availability, customer preference)?
18. How do you handle vehicle substitution if preferred vehicle is unavailable?
19. What vehicle information do customers need before accepting a rental?
20. How do you handle customer requests for specific vehicles?

### Check-Out Process
21. What is your standard vehicle check-out procedure?
22. What vehicle conditions or features do you inspect and document?
23. How do you document fuel level and mileage?
24. What safety briefings or instructions do you provide?
25. How do you verify customer identity and rental agreement understanding?

### Check-In & Return Process
26. What is your standard vehicle check-in procedure?
27. How do you inspect returned vehicles for damage?
28. What documentation is required during vehicle return?
29. How do you assess and document vehicle condition differences?
30. How long does the check-in process typically take?

### Damage Assessment & Claims
31. How do you assess and document vehicle damage?
32. What is your policy for damage responsibility and billing?
33. How do you determine if damage is pre-existing or caused during rental?
34. What is the damage claim process for customers?
35. How do you handle disputed damage claims?

### Mileage & Fuel Management
36. How do you handle mileage charges (unlimited vs. per-mile)?
37. What is your fuel policy (full to full, partial, fuel surcharge)?
38. How do you document mileage at check-out and check-in?
39. How do you handle fuel level discrepancies?
40. How do you calculate mileage charges for overages?

### Additional Services & Extras
41. What additional services or add-ons do you offer (GPS, child seats, etc.)?
42. How do you manage add-on inventory and availability?
43. How do you charge for additional services?
44. How do you track add-ons and ensure they're returned?

### Late Return & Extensions
45. How do you handle late vehicle returns?
46. What are your late return fees and policies?
47. How do you manage rental extensions?
48. How do you communicate return due dates to customers?

### Special Rental Types
49. Do you offer special rental types (one-way, corporate, long-term)?
50. How do you handle each rental type differently?
51. What special terms or pricing apply to special rental types?
52. How do you track and manage special rental agreements?

### Customer Communication & Documentation
53. What rental information do you provide to customers?
54. How do you communicate rental details to customers after booking?
55. How do you send reminders about return dates and times?
56. What documentation do customers receive?

### Compliance & Legal
57. What compliance requirements apply to rentals?
58. How do you ensure proper age and license verification?
59. What legal documentation or records must be retained?
60. How do you handle data privacy and security for customer information?

### Rental Analytics & Reporting
61. What rental metrics are important to track?
62. How do you analyze rental trends and patterns?
63. What reports do you need on rental performance?
64. How do you track rental revenue by vehicle, location, or customer segment?

### System Requirements & Integration
65. What rental-related data do you need to track in the system?
66. How do you want rental workflows to be managed in the system?
67. What integration with other systems is needed?
68. What mobile or field access is needed for rental staff?
69. What reports or dashboards would be most valuable?

### Exception Handling
70. How do you handle rental exceptions or unusual situations?
71. What escalation process exists for rental issues?
72. How do you handle customer disputes during the rental process?
73. What authority do rental staff have for exceptions?
