# Customer Support - Interview Questions

## Car Rental System Requirements Gathering

### Support Channels & Communication
1. What support channels do customers use to reach you (phone, email, chat, in-person)?
2. How do you prioritize support requests?
3. What is your typical response time for different types of inquiries?
4. How do you track and manage support tickets or requests?
5. Do you need integration with support ticketing systems?

### Common Customer Issues
6. What are the most common customer issues or inquiries you handle?
7. How do you resolve rental-related problems (vehicle condition, missing features)?
8. What issues require escalation to management?
9. How do you handle damage claims or dispute resolution?
10. What is your process for processing refunds or credits?

### Rental Inquiry Support
11. How do customers inquire about rental status or details?
12. What information do customers typically request?
13. How do you access rental information to assist customers?
14. How long does it typically take to locate and provide rental information?
15. Do you need real-time access to rental system information?

### Issue Documentation & Tracking
16. How do you document customer issues and resolutions?
17. What information is captured in a support case or incident record?
18. How do you track resolution status and follow-up actions?
19. What reporting do you need on support metrics?
20. How long do you need to retain customer support records?

### Knowledge Base & Self-Service
21. Do you have a knowledge base for common questions?
22. What would help reduce support ticket volume?
23. Are there common issues that customers could resolve themselves with proper information?
24. How do you keep support documentation up-to-date?
25. What FAQs or help topics are most needed?

### Customer Feedback & Improvement
26. How do you collect customer feedback on their experience?
27. How do you identify trends in customer issues?
28. How is customer feedback used to improve processes?
29. Do you track customer satisfaction with support interactions?
30. What improvements would most impact customer satisfaction?

### Special Requests & Accommodations
31. What types of special requests do customers make?
32. How do you handle special accommodations or exceptions?
33. What authority do you have to approve adjustments or waivers?
34. When do you need to escalate special requests?

### Post-Rental Support
35. Do you provide support after rental completion?
36. How do you handle billing disputes or billing inquiries?
37. What follow-up do you do for damage claims?
38. How do you handle customer feedback after rentals?

### Integration & System Requirements
39. What systems do you need access to provide support?
40. Would integration with rental, billing, and vehicle systems help?
41. What information would be most valuable to have quickly accessible?
42. Do you need mobile access to support customer inquiries?

### Performance & Metrics
43. What support metrics do you track (resolution time, satisfaction, volume)?
44. How do you measure support quality and effectiveness?
45. What KPIs are important for your support team?
46. How do you benchmark your support performance?

### Multilingual & International Support
47. Do you support customers in multiple languages?
48. What support requirements exist for international customers?
49. Are there regional differences in support needs?

### System Accessibility & Usability
50. How user-friendly does the system need to be for support staff?
51. What features would help support staff resolve issues faster?
52. What reporting or analytics tools would help your team?
